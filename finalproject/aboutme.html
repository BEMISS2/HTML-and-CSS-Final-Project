Behind the Design

Hi, I'm Koree.

I live on a farm with my father, mother, and sister. I have a dog, Kodyak and a cat, Pepper. I enjoy watching movies, listening to music, and spending time with my friends and family. I would love to one-day travel all around the U.S.A, especially New York City. I would even love to venture outside the country and go to Ireland.

My passion is graphic design. As a graphic designer, I have a strong grasp on designing promotional materials and uniquely fun graphics. I am an individual who values timelines and organization. I have a whimsical aspect to my art, and I am not afraid of color.

I am attending Northwestern Michigan College, once my education is completed I would like live in Northern Michigan and work where I can excel and use my skills to contribute artistically back to the community. Later on in life, I plan on advancing my career to become a Personal Graphic Designer and starting my own Graphic Design business. With my artistic abilities, I will be able to offer services to preserve my customer's greatest memories. Whether it is invitations for a family member's birthday party or posters to promote a local band, I'll be happy to help.
